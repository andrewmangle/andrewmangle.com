Finance

Compliance and risk tools for lenders, advisors, and finance teams

Regulated finance teams spend more time documenting decisions than making them. These modules turn manual review into a system your auditors and examiners can trust.

MODULE 01

AuditLine

Builds SEC-ready audit trails directly from your email, calendar, and trade data, so documentation stops being a quarterly fire drill.

Solves: manual compliance documentation for RIAs and broker-dealers under $500M AUM.

MODULE 02

LedgerSight

Connects to borrower bank feeds and produces rolling 90-day cash flow projections loan officers can act on, not stale tax returns.

Solves: underwriting decisions based on outdated financials at community banks and credit unions.

MODULE 03

ESG Ledger

Pulls public ESG signals into investor-ready reports, without an enterprise data contract.

Solves: ESG reporting requirements for mid-market asset managers without enterprise budgets.

MODULE 04

PayGuard

Flags anomalous ACH and wire patterns before they clear, layered on top of your existing accounting system.

Solves: business email compromise and vendor payment fraud for mid-size finance teams.

In the field

A four-advisor RIA cut audit prep from roughly three weeks to three days after routing client communications through AuditLine automatically.

Energy

Operational tools for energy buyers and field teams

Energy costs and maintenance schedules get managed in spreadsheets far longer than they should. These modules catch what spreadsheets miss.

MODULE 05

BillWatch

Reads incoming utility bills automatically and flags overbilling, rate misclassification, and meter errors before you pay them.

Solves: overpayment across portfolios of 10 to 50+ commercial properties.

MODULE 06

ProcureGrid

Models and compares 12 to 36 month supply contracts across deregulated markets side by side.

Solves: comparing competitive electricity contracts without an in-house trading desk.

MODULE 07

RECTrack

Tracks, verifies, and retires renewable energy certificates in one place, instead of a shared spreadsheet nobody fully trusts.

Solves: voluntary sustainability commitment tracking for mid-size companies.

MODULE 08

FieldCycle

Mobile-first scheduling for inverter checks, cleaning rotations, and warranty tracking across distributed solar sites.

Solves: O&M operators running sub-50MW solar portfolios on email and spreadsheets.

In the field

A regional O&M operator managing 35 solar sites eliminated missed maintenance windows within the first quarter of switching to FieldCycle.

Healthcare

Administrative tools for clinics and group practices

Clinical staff lose hours a day to administrative work that has nothing to do with patient care. These modules sit on top of your existing EMR, not in place of it.

MODULE 09

AuthFlow

Preps authorization documentation, tracks payer status, and alerts your team before a delay turns into a denied claim.

Solves: prior authorization burden at specialty clinics whose EMR wasn't built for it.

MODULE 10

CredWatch

A compliance calendar that tracks license, DEA, and payer credentialing dates so nothing lapses without warning.

Solves: revenue loss from credentialing gaps at multi-provider group practices.

MODULE 11

ShowSure

Flags high-risk no-shows 48 to 72 hours out using appointment history, insurance type, and demographics.

Solves: day-of cancellations that quietly drain ambulatory surgical center revenue.

MODULE 12

VitalsBrief

Turns raw glucose, blood pressure, and weight device streams into a one-page daily digest with outliers flagged.

Solves: physician overload from unfiltered remote patient monitoring data.

In the field

A twelve-provider dermatology group recovered an estimated 14 staff hours a week after automating prior authorization with AuthFlow.
