Your Resume

Share a link to your current resume — Google Drive, Dropbox, a personal site, anywhere it lives online. This signals that your resume is ready and accessible when opportunity calls. Not providing a resume link deducts 5 points from your Professionalism score.

⚠ No resume provided — 5 points deducted from Professionalism. This is one of the most common professional gaps Dr. Mangle sees. When opportunity shows up, students who are not ready lose it. A resume link costs you nothing to add. You can update this before submitting.
✓ Resume link received. Your Professionalism score reflects the full 15-point potential. Dr. Mangle's team will review your resume as part of the 48-hour reviewed score process.

Your E-Portfolio

Share a single link to your e-portfolio — a website, Notion page, GitHub, Google Site, or any page that aggregates your work, projects, certifications, and evidence. One link is all it takes. Students who provide a portfolio link typically receive higher reviewed scores because the evidence is visible. Optional but strongly encouraged.
